"Where do you want to eat?"

"I don't know. Where do you want to go?"

"I don't care. What are you in the mood for?"

Sound familiar? DineTime is a web application built to help get rid of this dialogue once and for all. To start, click on the logo and a restaurant will be randomly chosen for you. Or, check out the restaurant list below and add restaurants to your dine list by pressing the button underneath the restaurant.

Check out the green triangle on your left for more info. Use the search bar to find restaurants with desired characteristics (i.e. "dog friendly", "fries", "okonomiyaki").
